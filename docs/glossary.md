# Glossary

## Exchange
    An exchange is a regulated marketplace in which individuals can buy and sell financial commoditites to one another

## Matching Engine
    A system that matches buy orders with sell orders in order to process transactions

## Order Book
    A compilation of all buy and sell orders in an exchange

## Order
    A single intention to buy or sell a financial instrument at a specified price and quantity.

## Limit Order
    A type of order in which price is specified and is either the lowest someone is willing to sell or the highest they are willing to pay

## Market Order
    A type of order without regards to price, instead prioritizes immediacy of transaction

## Bid
    The highest price a buyer is willing to pay for a commodity

## Ask
    The lowest price a seller is willing to accept for a commodity

## Spread
    The difference between the ask and bid. Is used to determine market volatility and buyer vs seller perception

## Fill
    To complete an order through the matching engine by matching a bid order with a ask order.

## Partial Fill
    An order that is only partially completed because insufficient matching quantity exists on the opposing side.

## Price-Time Priority
    The idea that orders placed at the best price will be filed first, followed by the time in which orders at the same price are placed
